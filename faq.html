Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

Pickup Only — No Shipping

All orders are pickup only. Delaware Cottage Food License restricts us to local pickup — we cannot ship cookies.

Where do I pick up my order?

We offer pickup in Middletown, DE (free, our main location) and West Chester, PA. Wilmington and Dover pickups are available with a small convenience fee. Local delivery is available at a distance-based fee. Exact pickup address is emailed to you closer to your pickup date.

Do you ship?

No — we are unable to ship. Our Delaware Cottage Food License restricts all orders to local pickup only. We appreciate your understanding.

How far in advance do I need to order?

We require a 2–4 week lead time for all custom cookie orders. Rush orders placed within 7 days of the pickup date are available but incur a 25% rush surcharge.

What is the minimum order size?

The minimum order is 2 dozen cookies for all tiers. The exception is the "My Favorite Things" set, which has a 1-dozen minimum.

How does payment work?

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required within 48 hours of receiving your invoice — this secures your date. The remaining balance is due one week before your pickup date. Invoices not paid within 48 hours are automatically cancelled.

Can I change my order after placing it?

Theme and quantity changes are allowed within 3 days of your deposit. After that, your order is finalized and changes cannot be accommodated.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations are non-refundable. As an alternative, we are happy to reschedule your order to a future available date.

What happens if I don't pay my invoice on time?

If your invoice is not paid within 48 hours of receipt, your order will be automatically cancelled and the spot released.

Are rush orders available?

Yes — rush orders placed within 7 days of the pickup date are accepted subject to availability. A 25% rush surcharge is added to the total. Email info@bachatabakery.com to check availability first.
