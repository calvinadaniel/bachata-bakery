About Me

Born in the Bronx.
Raised on the flavors
of la isla.

Hi, I'm Raquel — the proud owner and founder of Bachata Bakery. I was born in the Bronx to Dominican and Puerto Rican parents, raised in a community full of music, family, and flavor. For me, dessert was never just dessert — a Dominican cake at a family gathering was a symbol of love, joy, and togetherness.

Every cookie, every cake, every cinnamon roll I bake carries that spirit with it. I want every order to feel like a celebration — like stepping into someone's abuela's kitchen in Santo Domingo.
